FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our services, process, and pricing.

Vaishno Devi Planning Centre (VDPC) is a government approval facilitation company based in Saroornagar, Hyderabad, Telangana. Founded by Abhilashh Goud Jajula in 2010, we act as a trusted intermediary between property owners and government authorities. Our licensed engineers and architects handle all paperwork, plan preparation, and government submissions for Building Permissions, LRS, TDR, and architectural designs.
Building Permissions typically take 14–30 days through our platform, compared to 45–90 days when done independently. LRS applications take 30–60 days depending on government workload. TDR processing takes 20–45 days. We always provide an estimated timeline before you begin. Our 98% success rate means very few cases face rejection or delays.
For Building Permission you typically need: (1) Pattadar Passbook / Sale Deed, (2) Aadhaar Card, (3) Encumbrance Certificate (EC), (4) Survey Map / Field Measurement Book, (5) Property Tax Receipts, (6) NOC from competent authority if applicable, and (7) Ownership documents. Our team will guide you on the exact requirements based on your plot location and type.
No. Our service fee covers professional services (plan preparation, documentation, submission, follow-up). Government fees such as application fees, betterment charges, scrutiny fees, and stamp duties are additional and vary based on plot area, location, and type of approval. We provide a full cost breakup including estimated government charges before you proceed.
